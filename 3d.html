Scrapgun

Medium: 3D Graphics (Autodesk Maya)


The Scrapgun is based on Ork Shoota designs from Warhammer 40,000. Its rough and blocky construction made it simple to recreate in 3d using the box modelling method. I decided to try a low-poly approach with this model, meaning it is created with as few polygons as possible.

Punk Head

Medium: 3D Graphics (Autodesk Maya)


This punk head is an exercise in modelling a human head and developing a personality, as well as unwrapping and texturing an organic shape.

Jumper

Medium: 3D Graphics (Autodesk Maya)


A step up from the Punk Head, the Jumper is an exercise in creating an entire humanoid model. Though it is not visible in a browser, this model is fully rigged and ready for posing and/or animating.
