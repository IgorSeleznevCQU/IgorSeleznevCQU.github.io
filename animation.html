Various Exercises

Medium: Frame-by-Frame Animation (Adobe Photoshop), Digital Animation (Adobe After Effects)


These animations are an exercise in developing fluid movement in different media - the animations created in Adobe Photoshop are representative of the original style of animation, where every frame is drawn by hand, whereas the Adobe After Effects animations are created by assigning keyframes to individual elements' properties, such as position, scale, and rotation. The action in these animations is somewhat exaggerated and shows plenty of squash & stretch, overlapping action, and follow through to make the animation more readable.

These animations were created as part of the Digital Media course at CQUniversity.
